Key Guidelines for Consultants
Income Buffers

Always apply a 15% buffer on gross income for EPF/SOCSO deductions if net income is not provided.

CCRIS/CTOS

Any '1' or '2' status in the last 6 months requires a strong deviation letter for top-tier banks.

Urban NDI

Properties in Klang Valley, Penang, and JB require an additional RM 150-250 NDI buffer.

Joint Applicants

NDI is calculated collectively for joint applications, but DSR is assessed against combined commitments.
